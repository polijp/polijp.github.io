Jean-Philippe Poli

Researcher · Commissariat à l'Energie Atomique et aux Energies Alternatives · · +33 1 69 08 78 56
Teacher · CentraleSupélec ·

Enthusiastic about Artificial Intelligence since the youngest age, I studied Mathematics and Computer Science until my specialization in AI and my PhD at University of Marseilles and French National Audiovisual Institute (defended in 2007).

After a two-year postdoctoral fellowship at CEA, I've been hired as permanent researcher. My research interests are symbolic AI, knowledge representation, reasoning, interpretability and explanabilty. My postdoctoral fellowship at CEA List lead to two different software platforms called ExpressIF (a Symbolic Artificial Intelligence) and PACT (a set of tools that extract symbols from raw data). I'm currently supervising the scientific and technical works of these two platforms.

I'm also teacher at CentraleSupélec (formerly Ecole Centrale Paris) since 2004.
